Arnold Schwarzenegger

Lifting legend & movie hero


Here is a timeline of Arnie's rise to fame:

Arnold Schwarzenegger always had the drive to do more, he was was born on July 30, 1947. A young austrian boy who used to play soccer. When his soccer coach decided to take the team to a local gym, this is where Arnie gained a desire to carry on lifting.

His father sent him to the austrian army and he began training with the equipment there. During his time there he snuck out to compete at the Junior Mr. Europe and managed to win despite no official gym equipment.

Strength does not come from winning. Your struggles develop your strengths. When you go through hardships and decide not to surrender, that is strength
